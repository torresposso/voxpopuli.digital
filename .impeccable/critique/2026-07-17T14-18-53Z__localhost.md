## Anti-Patterns Verdict

* **LLM Assessment**: The current hero block has a solid dark blue background that, while on-brand, feels visually heavy and lacks depth. The typography is a major deviation from the brandbook: the title uses a sans-serif font instead of the elegant `Playfair Display 800`, and the excerpt uses a sans-serif instead of `Literata`. There is also no button/CTA to read the article. The image of Alcides Arrieta on the right has a flat white background, creating a stark rectangular contrast with the dark background rather than integrating organically.
* **Deterministic Scan**: The theme's style sheet contains multiple direct font-size and color overrides that drift away from the design system definitions in `DESIGN.md`.

## Overall Impression

The hero section achieves good contrast and brand identification (using Azul Caribe #2f2982), but feels unfinished and flat due to missing typography hierarchy, lack of a clear call-to-action button, and poor visual blending of the featured image against the dark background.

## What's Working
* **Color and Contrast**: The Azul Caribe background offers excellent contrast against the white text, complying with WCAG readability guidelines.
* **Basic Layout**: The two-column grid distributes text and imagery in a clean, traditional way.

## Priority Issues

* **[P1] Typography Mismatch**: The title and excerpt do not use the fonts specified in `DESIGN.md` (`Playfair Display` for title and `Literata` for excerpt).
  * *Why it matters*: Undermines the editorial identity and visual prestige of the site, making it feel like a generic SaaS template rather than a premium journalism portal.
  * *Fix*: Apply `font-family: Playfair Display` to the heading and `font-family: Literata` to the excerpt.
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable typeset`

* **[P1] Missing Call to Action (CTA)**: No "Leer expediente" outline button is present in the layout.
  * *Why it matters*: Users are forced to figure out that the block or title is clickable; lacks a clear signpost for interaction.
  * *Fix*: Insert the primary outline button "Leer expediente" below the excerpt as specified in `DESIGN.md`.
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable layout`

* **[P2] Image Integration**: The featured image has a hard white background which clashes visually with the dark blue background.
  * *Why it matters*: Disrupts the aesthetic quality, making the image look like an unblended box stuck on top of the hero.
  * *Fix*: Apply a subtle mix-blend-mode or shadow, or adjust the container to handle images with solid backgrounds gracefully (e.g. transparent PNGs or container wrapper styling).
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable polish`

* **[P2] Flat Background Lack of Depth**: The solid blue background is extremely flat.
  * *Why it matters*: Feels static and visually uninteresting for a premium brand hero.
  * *Fix*: Introduce a very subtle pattern, fine border line separators, or a soft radial background gradient.
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable colorize`

## Persona Red Flags

* **Jordan (First-Timer)**: Will look at the hero section and read the text, but won't immediately realize they can click the title to read the article because there is no button or interactive indicator (like an arrow or "Leer más").
* **Riley (Stress Tester)**: Will check how the hero responds on mobile and tablet. Without fluid typography and correct flex wrapping, the large text on the dark background will overflow or occupy too much vertical space.
