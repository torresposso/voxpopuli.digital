## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM Assessment**: The layout is a standard three-column hero layout with a background image stretching across the container, but it suffers from a major contrast failure. The text is pure black (`rgb(0,0,0)`) on a dark overlay, which is a classic training-data default styling bug where local theme text defaults override container-specific contrast schemes.

**Deterministic Scan**: The automated detector scanned theme assets and noted standard HTML structure, but found no structural syntax blockers. The contrast ratio itself is the primary visual blocker.

## Overall Impression

The hero section has a strong editorial promise, but the visual execution falls flat due to the black text color over the dark background image overlay and the distracting blurry panels. Fixing the text contrast and alignment will instantly elevate the home page to a professional level.

## What's Working
- **Structure**: The division of content and the layout structure are logical.
- **Header Integration**: The header sits cleanly above the hero section.

## Priority Issues

- **[P0] Headline Contrast and Readability**: The main headline text (`Cinco días de bloqueo...`) is rendered in pure black (`rgb(0, 0, 0)`) over a dark background image with a dark overlay, resulting in a contrast ratio near 1:1.
  * *Why it matters*: Users cannot read the primary story of the website, which completely defeats the purpose of the hero section and violates WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility guidelines.
  * *Fix*: Apply a white text color override (`#ffffff`) to `.post-title` and post meta elements within the hero section.
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable polish`
- **[P1] Blurry Side Panel Mirroring**: The background image uses a container-wide stretch with a blurry mirroring effect on the right panel, which looks visually messy.
  * *Why it matters*: It distracts from the main featured image in the center and makes the page feel unpolished.
  * *Fix*: Confine the featured image to a clean 16:9 or 16:10 aspect ratio rounded card on the right, and use a high-contrast solid background for the text column on the left (following the canonical `DESIGN.md` rules).
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable layout`
- **[P2] Font Styling**: The main title font does not match the bold, editorial serif brand persona.
  * *Why it matters*: The typography lacks the voice and presence expected from a leading progressive news outlet.
  * *Fix*: Set the font-family of the title to the official brand serif font.
  * *Suggested command*: `$impeccable typeset`

## Persona Red Flags

**Jordan (First-Timer)**: Arrives at the homepage and cannot read the main headline. Will struggle to understand what the featured story is and might assume the site is broken or unfinished.

**Sam (Accessibility-Dependent)**: Completely blocked from reading the headline visually. Screen readers will read it, but low-vision users tabbing through will find it completely invisible.
